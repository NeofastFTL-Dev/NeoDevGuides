# TechRevised

TechRevised is a technology progression mod focused on automation and machines.

---

## Download
https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/techrevised

---

## Core Idea
You start with simple manual machines and advance into full automation factories.

The mod is designed to:
- Extend early-game tech
- Provide long-term progression
- Integrate into modpacks easily

---

## Typical Progression
1. Basic Machines
2. Power Generation
3. Processing Machines
4. Automation
5. Advanced Systems

---

## Tips
- Automate early
- Always store excess power
- Combine with storage mods (AE2 / Refined Storage)
