# OreTrees

Grow your resources instead of mining them.

OreTrees allows players to farm ores using special trees that produce **acorns, resin, and amber**.

---

## Download
https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/oretrees

---

## Getting Started

1. Craft an Ore Sapling
2. Place it on dirt or farmland
3. Wait for it to grow
4. Harvest:
   - Acorns
   - Resin
   - Amber

Combine materials to craft ingots.

---

## Materials

### Acorns
Primary crafting material used for resource recipes.

### Resin
Binding material required for ore crafting.

### Amber
Advanced material used for higher tier recipes.

---

## Progression
Wood → Iron → Gold → Diamond → Netherite

OreTrees replaces early mining and encourages automation farms.

---
